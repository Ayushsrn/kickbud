About

To Kickbud is to keep the Kickbud off the floor. The Kickbud is aerodynamically designed to improve flight time and landing, making it easy for beginners. Improves foot-eye coordination, reflexes, first touches and mood

>

How to Play

The aim of the game is to keep the Kickbud off the floor. The game can be played solo, with a partner, or with a team of up to 8 people.

Solo players can juggle the Kickbud and show off their freestyle skills. 2-4 players can group themselves and play competitively to see who lets it drop first. 2-8 players can stand in a circle to freestyle and keep the Kickbud in air for as long as possible.

  • Things to carry – Kickbud
  • Where to play – Indoor or Outdoor

PS – Avoid extremely windy places
