Frequently Asked Questions

1 - What is a Kickbud?

The Kickbud is the 21st-century upgrade to the traditional shuttlecock. It has 4 feathers arranged adjacently and attached to a weighted rubber base.

2 - How to play the game?

The aim is to keep the Kickbud off the floor. The player can use their feet, chest, head, shoulders and even hands (depending on what is agreed upon).

3 - How many players are needed to Kickbud?

Kickbud can be played alone, or with up to 8 players. A solo player can juggle the Kickbud, freestyle and mix it up. 2-4 players can make teams, place a net between them and play competitively to see who lets the Kickbud touch the ground first. 2-8 players can form one team to see how many touches they can make as a team before the Kickbud hits the floor.

4 - Where can this game be played?

Kickbud is indoor-friendly. The game can be played in a basement, a living room, a terrace, a soccer field, a badminton court… basically ANYWHERE! However, it is advisable to avoid playing in extremely windy locations for a better experience.
